The New Silkroad

Japan's secondary market holds some of the world's most immaculate timepieces — kept in conditions European collectors rarely encounter, often unworn, and frequently unobtainable through conventional channels. At Higashi Koen, our relationships open the door to this market. We know it deeply and have built our business around bringing it directly to you.

Our export channel was designed with one goal: to transfer these watches from Japan to Europe smoothly and without compromise. For collectors who know what they want, we handle everything else.
